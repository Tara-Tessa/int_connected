intro

This app is designed for those people who
feel like they’re missing someone in their life.

a Soulmate

We help those people to find their soulmate
with a special kind of necklace that will lead
you to your other half.

There are three types of soulmates: a five
star soulmate, a four star soulmate and a
three star soulmate.

A five star soulmate is your partner in life. Emotionely, sexualy, spiritualy,… It’s your boyfriend or girfliend, husband or wife.

The four star soulmate is actually your best friend in life. You share happy memories together and enjoy each others company.

The last kind of soulmate, a three star soulmate, is just a friend. When you feel lonely you two can go grab a drink together, but not much more than that.

Based on the type of soulmate you want to
find, a kind of metal gets chosen. Five star
soulmates get gold, four star soulmates get
silver and three star soulmates get rose gold.

There are thousands and thousands of
different kinds of gemstones with each a
personality that is linked with it. Here some
examples.

If your personality is linked to the amethyst you are a multi-talented person. People often like you because of your honesty. You’re also a very courageous person.

People who have a green emerald as there personality stone are very intelligent. They are also known to be a little quick-witted because of their strong and mostly right intuition.

Topaz-linked people are really friendly and fair-minded. They are very flexible in a lot of situations due to their great mental strength.

After your kind of metal and stone are
chosen, it’s time to see if there are any
matches with other people who are looking
for a soulmate. When there is a match the
gemstone gets prepared to fit into
the necklace itself and then gets broken in
two so that each person gets a half.

And just like that you have the key to find
your soulmate, your other half.

To make it easier to find that significant
other you can use the tracking option in this
app or go to one of the soulmate events.
There you can easily find your other half
and celebrate other people’s happiness.
